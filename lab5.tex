$\Rightarrow P_{\mathrm {R2} }=I_{\mathrm {R2} }*V_{\mathrm {R2} }=0.001447*0.68=0.984 mw $ \\[1em]

$P_{\mathrm {R3} }=I_{\mathrm {D} }^2*R_{\mathrm {3} }=0.001^2*100=0.1 mw $
